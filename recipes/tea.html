Tea

Description

This lemon tea is my favorite tea. It is a sweet and sour tea â€” you taste the honey, then the lemon. Great for soothing sore throats. Yum, yum, yum!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour water into a mug. Add honey and heat in the microwave for 1 minute 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in lemon juice, mixing until honey is dissolved. Stir in sugar.
